Overview

Purpose

This website aims to provide visitors with a variety of healthy and delicious recipes. Each recipe will include a list of ingredients, step-by-step instructions, photos of the finished dish and preparation tips. In addition, special sections for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and other specific diets will be included.

Audience

The target audience is people interested in healthy cooking and those looking to incorporate more nutritious options into their daily diet. This may include people following specific diets, those looking to lose weight or simply those who want to eat healthier.

Content

Home page

Header: An attractive header that includes the name of my website and a slogan that summarizes what my site is about. Navigation menu: A clear, easy-to-use menu that allows visitors to navigate through the different sections of my site. Main banner: A main banner with an attractive image and a powerful message to help capture the attention of visitors. It could be a picture of one of the most popular recipes or a healthy dish. Introduction: A brief introduction to explain the purpose of my site and what visitors can expect to find related to healthy and delicious cooking. This section should be attractive and persuasive to keep visitors interested. Images: Attractive images of finished dishes, images of fresh and nutritious ingredients, perhaps an image of a clean and well-organized kitchen to represent the atmosphere of a healthy kitchen to attract visitors and give them an idea of what they can achieve with the recipes. Recipe type sections: Highlight special sections for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and other specific diets. This will help visitors quickly find recipes that fit their dietary needs. Footer: A footer with additional information, such as privacy policy and terms of service, this is essential to provide visitors with important information about my site. Social media links: Links to my social media profiles so visitors can follow me and share content.

Images for the Home page

Recipe page

Header: The same as the home page. Navigation menu: A clear and easy to use menu that allows visitors to navigate through the different sections of my site. Main banner: A main banner with an attractive image and a powerful message to help capture visitors' attention. It can be an image of one of the most popular recipes or a healthy dish. Introduction: A brief introduction to explain the purpose of the healthy recipe page. Recipe title: Each recipe should start with an attractive and descriptive title. The title should be clear and concise, but also catch the reader's attention. Recipe yield: I will indicate how many servings each recipe yields. This is especially useful for those who are planning a meal for a specific number of people. I can also include the serving size, if applicable. Recipe image: An attractive image of the finished recipe can make recipes more appealing to visitors. Images should be of high quality and clearly show the final result of the recipe. Ingredient List: Each recipe should include a complete list of ingredients. The ingredients must have accurate quantities. Step-by-step instructions: Instructions for each recipe should be clear and easy to follow. They should be organized in numbered steps and each step should be as detailed as possible. Preparation and cooking time: It is important to include the total preparation and cooking time for each recipe. This helps visitors plan their time in the kitchen. I can also include the resting or marinating time, if necessary. Suggestion: I will include any tips or tricks that may help visitors succeed in making the recipe. This could include tips on cooking techniques, cooking utensil recommendations, or suggestions for recipe variations. Footer: A footer with additional information, such as privacy policy and terms of service, this is essential to provide visitors with important information about my site. Social media links: Links to my social media profiles so visitors can follow me and share content.

Images for the Recipe page

Special diets page

Header: Same as the home page. Navigation menu: A clear and easy to use menu that allows visitors to navigate through the different sections of my site. Introduction of the types of diets: An image of vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free food and a brief introduction to explain the purpose of each type of recipe. Recipes for each special diet: As with the recipes page, each recipe should include a title, how many people it is for and preparation time, an image, a list of ingredients, step-by-step instructions. Tips or tricks for each special diet. This could include tips on how to substitute certain ingredients. Footer: A footer with additional information, such as privacy policy and terms of service, this is essential to provide visitors with important information about my site. Social media links: Links to my social media profiles so visitors can follow me and share content.
