Dilan Conde

WDD 130-A3

Overview

Purpose

This website aims to provide visitors with a variety of healthy and delicious recipes. Each recipe will include a list of ingredients, step-by-step instructions, photos of the finished dish and preparation tips. In addition, special sections for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and other specific diets will be included.

Audience

The target audience is people interested in healthy cooking and those looking to incorporate more nutritious options into their daily diet. This may include people following specific diets, those looking to lose weight or simply those who want to eat healthier.

Content

Home page

Header: An attractive header that includes the name of my website and a slogan that summarizes what my site is about. Navigation menu: A clear, easy-to-use menu that allows visitors to navigate through the different sections of my site. Main banner: A main banner with an attractive image and a powerful message to help capture the attention of visitors. It could be a picture of one of the most popular recipes or a healthy dish. Introduction: A brief introduction to explain the purpose of my site and what visitors can expect to find related to healthy and delicious cooking. This section should be attractive and persuasive to keep visitors interested. Images: Attractive images of finished dishes, images of fresh and nutritious ingredients, perhaps an image of a clean and well-organized kitchen to represent the atmosphere of a healthy kitchen to attract visitors and give them an idea of what they can achieve with the recipes. Recipe type sections: Highlight special sections for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and other specific diets. This will help visitors quickly find recipes that fit their dietary needs. Social media links: Links to my social media profiles so visitors can follow me and share content. Footer: A footer with additional information such as privacy policy and terms of service, this is essential in order to provide visitors with important information about my site.

Images for the Home page

Recipe page

Recipe title: Each recipe should begin with an attractive and descriptive title. The title will be clear and concise, but will also capture the reader's attention. Recipe image: An attractive image of the finished recipe can make recipes more appealing to visitors. Images should be of high quality and clearly show the end result of the recipe. I consider including several images that show the steps of the recipe as well as the final presentation. Ingredient list: Each recipe should include a complete list of ingredients. Ingredients should be clearly listed and quantities should be accurate. It is also helpful to include any ingredient substitutions for those with dietary restrictions. You may consider including links to specific products or brands that you recommend. Step-by-step instructions: Instructions for each recipe should be clear and easy to follow. They should be organized into numbered steps and each step should be as detailed as possible. I consider including images for each step. Preparation and cooking time: It is important to include the total preparation and cooking time for each recipe. This helps visitors plan their time in the kitchen. I can also include the resting or marinating time, if necessary. Recipe yield: I will indicate how many servings each recipe yields. This is especially useful for those who are planning a meal for a specific number of people. I can also include the serving size, if relevant. Nutritional information: If possible, I will include nutritional information for each recipe. This can be especially useful for those who are following a specific diet or who are trying to track their calorie intake. Nutritional information can include calories, grams of protein, grams of carbohydrates, grams of fat, etc. Tips and tricks: I will include any tips or tricks that can help visitors be successful in making the recipe. This could include tips on cooking techniques, cooking utensil recommendations, or suggestions for recipe variations. I may also include notes on how to store or freeze the recipe, if relevant. Social sharing buttons: I will include social sharing buttons so that visitors can easily share the website with their friends and family. This can help increase the visibility of my site and attract new visitors.

Images for the Recipe page

Special diets page

Introduction to special diets: This section should provide an overview of the different special diets that my site covers, such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free. A brief explanation of what each diet is and why some people may choose to follow it. Recipes for each special diet: As with the recipes page, each recipe should include a title, an image, a list of ingredients, step-by-step instructions, preparation and cooking time, recipe yield, and nutritional information. Tips and tricks for each special diet: This section should provide tips and tricks for following each special diet. This could include tips on how to substitute certain ingredients, how to make sure you get all the necessary nutrients, and how to handle situations such as eating out or attending partie
